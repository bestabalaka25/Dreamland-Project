    Tartan Army

    NO SCOTLAND, NO PARTY

    From Wembley in 1977 to France 98, the Tartan Army turned Scotland's football journey into a travelling celebration of song, colour, and hope. Win or lose, the supporters made every tournament feel bigger than the scoreboard.

    “No Scotland, no party” became a rallying cry for generations of fans who followed the team across Europe and beyond, bringing blue shirts, bagpipes, flags, and unstoppable energy.

    Supporters' Motto

    “No Scotland, No Party”

    A chant that sums up the spirit of the Tartan Army — football as a celebration, whatever the score.
